Replication Script

Replication log for Margaret J. Foster “Subject to Change: Quantifying Transformation in Armed Conflict Actors At Scale Using Text” Last updated: December 19, 2023

The project extends Desiree Nilsson & Isak Svensson’s “The Intractability of Islamist Insurgencies: Islamist Rebels and the Recurrence of Civil War” with an original variable capturing observer perceptions of changes in militant organizations. As such, the analysis closely follows their replication scripts, with the addition of a new variable. This script produces Figure 6 of the main Subject to Change manuscript and Figures 11 and 14 of the Subject to Change Supplemental Appendix.
